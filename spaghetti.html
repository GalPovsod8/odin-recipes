SPAGETTHI

SPAGETTHI DESCRIPTION

Traditional, full-flavored spagehtti are layered together in just minutes with pasta sauce, noodles, and three cheeses.

Ingredients

  • 1 (26 ounce) jar Prego® Traditional Italian Sauce
  • 6 each uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients
  2. Put in the owen
  3. Now bake
  4. Your spagetthi are ready
